HOW WE WORK

Our Quote & Production Process

1. Site visit or measurements

We begin with a site visit if required, or the customer provides accurate sizes. For vehicles or shop signage we measure the surfaces needed for a precise quote.

2. Quote preparation

Sizes are essential for a reliable quote. Once the details are confirmed we prepare a professional proposal that reflects scope, materials and installation needs.

3. Quote acceptance

Acceptance of quote is only to info@signworx.co.za or 064 075 2497. Quotes from R0.00 to R300.00 are payable in full on acceptance. Quotes above R300.00 require a 70% deposit on acceptance before the artwork phase begins.

4. Artwork approval

After the deposit is received, the job moves to our graphic designer. The design is sent to the client for approval before production starts.

5. Production

Once the artwork is approved, production begins with premium materials, in-house fabrication and quality control.

6. Installation planning

If installation or application is required, the client is informed of availability and our installation team coordinates the site visit.
